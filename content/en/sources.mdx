To reduce mistakes, we prioritize **primary sources**: official pricing/fees pages, terms, help center articles, and risk disclosures.

## Source types (priority)

1. **Official pricing/fees** pages (fees, spreads, FX conversion, withdrawals).
2. **Terms & risk disclosures** (what you’re buying, CFD/crypto warnings).
3. **Help center** (KYC, deposits/withdrawals, limits).
4. **Security pages** (2FA, protections, account recovery).
5. **Registries / authorities** when applicable (entity, warnings).

## “2-minute verification” method

Before acting (deposit, trade, copy, withdraw), check:

- the document **date** (and whether a local version exists for your country)
- whether the page is about the **asset** or a **CFD**
- the **total cost** (explicit fees + spread + FX conversion)
- the **withdrawal** conditions (delay, fees, required docs)
