<h2>Privacy policy</h2>
<p>This privacy policy explains how <strong>trading-verifie.com</strong> collects, uses, stores and protects personal data, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) No. 2016/679.</p>

<h2>1. Data controller</h2>
<p><strong>Publisher:</strong> E-Com Shop<br/>
<strong>Publication manager:</strong> Tony Caron<br/>
<strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com">contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com</a></p>

<h2>2. Data we collect</h2>
<p>We only collect data that is strictly necessary to provide our services:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Email address</strong> via the newsletter form</li>
<li><strong>Browsing data</strong> via cookies (analytics, language preferences)</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Purposes</h2>
<p>Personal data is collected for the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sending a free PDF guide by email</li>
<li>Occasional newsletters related to affiliation (e.g., eToro, Bitpanda) or content updates</li>
<li>Improving user experience (preferences, translations)</li>
<li>Anonymous traffic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics 4)</li>
</ul>

<h2>4. Legal basis</h2>
<p>Data processing is based exclusively on the user’s <strong>explicit consent</strong>. No processing is carried out for commercial purposes without your prior consent.</p>

<h2>5. Retention period</h2>
<p>Email addresses are kept until you unsubscribe. Anonymous browsing data is kept for up to 14 months (e.g., via analytics tools).</p>

<h2>6. Processors</h2>
<p>Collected data may be processed through the following services:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Google Sheets</strong> (newsletter form storage)</li>
<li><strong>Google Analytics 4</strong> (traffic analysis)</li>
<li><strong>Netlify</strong> (secure static hosting)</li>
</ul>

<h2>7. Data security</h2>
<p>Data is stored in secure environments. No sensitive data is collected or stored on the site’s server.</p>

<h2 id="cookies">8. Cookies</h2>
<p>Our site may use cookies such as:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Language cookie</strong> (translation preferences)</li>
<li><strong>Consent cookie</strong> (cookie banner)</li>
<li><strong>Analytics cookies</strong> (enabled only after consent)</li>
</ul>
<p>You can manage or delete cookies at any time in your browser settings.</p>

<h2>9. Your rights</h2>
<p>In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Right of access</strong> to your personal data</li>
<li><strong>Right to rectification</strong> if data is inaccurate</li>
<li><strong>Right to erasure</strong> (“right to be forgotten”)</li>
<li><strong>Right to data portability</strong></li>
<li><strong>Right to object</strong> or to withdraw consent at any time</li>
</ul>
<p>You can exercise these rights by writing to: <a href="mailto:contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com">contact.ecomshopfrance@gmail.com</a></p>

<h2>10. Complaints</h2>
<p>If you believe your rights are not respected, you can file a complaint with the French data protection authority <strong>CNIL</strong> via <a href="https://www.cnil.fr">www.cnil.fr</a>.</p>

<h2>11. Policy updates</h2>
<p>We may update this privacy policy at any time. The last update date is displayed on the page.</p>
