The site’s goal is simple: help readers avoid **costly mistakes** before depositing meaningful money on a platform.

We don’t publish market signals. We focus on:

- what you’re actually buying (asset vs CFD, leverage, restrictions)
- what it costs (fees + spreads + FX conversion)
- what can block you (withdrawals, KYC, country limits)
- what can go wrong (product risk, platform risk, user risk)

## “Before you deposit” checklist

Before you scale, verify:

1. **Product type**: real asset, CFD, or a mix depending on country/account settings.
2. **Fees**: deposit/withdrawal, FX conversion, spreads, inactivity, custody.
3. **Withdrawals**: delay, method, fees, documents, min/max.
4. **KYC & country rules**: restrictions, unavailable assets, leverage limits.
5. **Security**: 2FA, alerts, protections, incident history (when public).
6. **Transparency**: accessible docs, clear risk warnings, reachable support.

## What we test (when possible)

When possible, we prefer a small “real-life” test:

- create account + enable 2FA
- make a **small deposit**, then a **small withdrawal**
- compare announced vs observed friction (KYC, delays, fees)

Results vary by country, currency and payment method. We call it out when known.

## Updates

We update pages when:

- pricing/terms change
- a major feature changes (copy features, safety, withdrawals)
- an info change could lead to a **bad decision**

Update dates are shown at the top of pages.

## Disclaimer

This content is educational and not investment advice. CFDs and crypto are high-risk products; never risk money you can’t afford to lose.
