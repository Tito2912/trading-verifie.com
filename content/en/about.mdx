This site publishes **practical guides** (and comparisons) to understand platforms like eToro or Bitpanda: how they work, what to verify, key fees, and risk warnings.

We prefer a checklist approach: the goal is to avoid common mistakes (wrong product type, hidden friction, withdrawal surprises, misunderstanding risk).

## Affiliate transparency

Some pages may include **affiliate links**. If you use them, we may earn a commission (at no extra cost to you).  
This helps fund maintenance (writing, updates, hosting).

Our editorial line is simple:

- no income promises
- risk-first (CFDs, leverage, crypto) + friction-first (fees, FX, withdrawals)
- process over “tips”

## Updates & corrections

Platforms change fast (pricing, terms, country restrictions).  
When important info changes, we update the relevant page and show an update date.

If you spot an error or outdated info, email us and we’ll fix it.
