Stocks & ETFs: what “Real Securities” implies

Bitpanda states these orders are executed on a regulated exchange (market rules, liquidity, trading hours) — unlike crypto (often 24/7).

  • Market closed: you can’t buy/sell until the venue is open again.
  • Price can change between preview and execution (normal market behaviour).
  • Unexpected moves can reflect events (dividends, splits, mergers…)

Security & account: practical points

What Bitpanda states

Almost the entire crypto reserve is stored offline (cold storage), with session controls and email confirmations for sensitive actions.

What you should do

Enable 2FA, use a unique password, secure your email account and stay alert to phishing.

KYC & compliance

Depending on regulation and services, identity verification (KYC) is required. Compliance checks can impact deposits/withdrawals.

Bitpanda Card: eligibility & fees

Availability

Bitpanda states availability in eurozone countries (exceptions: Cyprus & Liechtenstein) and management via the mobile app.

Stated costs

ATM withdrawals: 2% of the withdrawal or minimum €2. Each payment triggers a conversion (trade) with the usual trading fees.

What to check

Limits, settings and possible extra fees: check the app. Some ATMs may add their own fees.

Eligibility (country / documents): essentials

Bitpanda states, among other things: 18+, a valid identity document, and residence in a supported country. Products and availability vary by country.

Bitpanda also states that people with US citizenship or located in the United States may not become users (regulatory reasons).

FAQ

Is Bitpanda suitable for beginners?

The interface is accessible, but crypto assets remain risky. Start small and keep learning.

What are the risks of crypto assets?

Volatility can lead to rapid losses. Only invest what you can afford to lose.

What fees should I expect on crypto?

Bitpanda states the cost is shown before confirmation. The stated fee range per trade is 0.00% to 2.49% depending on the asset (e.g., Bitcoin 0.99% buy & sell).

What is the minimum amount to start?

Bitpanda states a minimum of €10 (or equivalent) for fiat deposits/withdrawals and €1 (or equivalent) for crypto buy/sell/swap.

Does Bitpanda offer stocks and ETFs?

Yes, via Real Stocks & ETFs (Real Securities) depending on your country. Bitpanda states market hours (Mon–Fri 07:30–23:00, not 24/7) and that a spread (bid/ask) can apply.

How does the Bitpanda Card work and what fees apply?

Bitpanda states ATM withdrawals cost 2% of the withdrawal (minimum €2) and that a payment triggers a conversion (trade) with the usual trading fee. Check limits and settings in the app.

Do I need identity verification (KYC)?

Depending on your country and the services used, identity verification may be required. Bitpanda also lists age, document and residency requirements.

Is Bitpanda available in my country?

Availability and the offering vary by country. Bitpanda also states that people with US citizenship or located in the United States may not become users.
