Yes, the interface is clear and CopyTrading can help start with simple rules. However, apply rigorous risk management.

Does 0% commission mean there are no fees?

No. It removes commission on real stocks, but other fees may apply (spreads, conversions, withdrawals…). Always read the pricing grid.

Does CopyTrading guarantee profits?

No. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Diversify, follow a plan, and limit leverage.
