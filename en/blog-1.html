CopyTrading™: learn while investing

CopyTrading is one of eToro's flagship features, attracting many beginners. It's a system that allows automatically copying the portfolios and movements of the platform's best traders.

How does it work?

You browse a catalog of investor profiles with their historical performance, risk level, type of assets they trade (crypto, stocks, forex…), their average holding period, etc. With one click, you can invest an amount (e.g.: €100) to copy all their trades in real time.

A powerful educational tool

By copying an experienced trader, you follow their movements and receive notifications about their decisions. This helps understand investment logic, learn by observing, and train without spending hours analyzing markets.

A solution to generate passive income

CopyTrading is also an excellent way to generate semi-automated returns, provided you select serious, consistent and well-rated profiles. You can always stop, withdraw your funds or change copied trader at any time.
